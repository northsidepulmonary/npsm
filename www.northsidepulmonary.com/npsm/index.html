Welcome to the website of Northside Pulmonary & Sleep Medicine.

We specialize in treating patients with lung related problems (pulmonary medicine), sleep disorders (sleep medicine) and the critically ill such as those in the ICU (Critical Care medicine). Our physicians are board certified in these specialities.

Our focus is not just in treating the immediate problem, but, in collaboration with your primary physician, working with you to ensure long term good pulmonary & sleep health and in assisting you make the right healthy choices in life.


Our main clinic is located in Northside Forsyth Hospital in Cumming. We have satellite clinics in Dawsonville and Buford/Sugar Hill.
