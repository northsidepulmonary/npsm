Services

Our physicians are board certified in pulmonary, critical care and sleep medicine and specialize in treating patients with lung related or sleep related disorders and diseases.

Some of the lung related diseases & disorders that we specialize in treating include COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease), Pulmonary Emphysema, Asthma, Pnuemonia, Tuberculosis, Lung cancer, cystic fibrosis, pulmonary hypertension, chronic bronchitis, occupational lung diseases, sarcoidosis and cough.

Sleep disorders & diseases include insomnia, narcolepsy, restless leg syndrome, obstructive sleep apnea, hypersomnia, teeth grinding, jet lag and sleepwalking.
